Building Interactive Experience with Tracking





I made an online shopping website, with a mouse and eye gaze tracking going at the same time, that users can act as customers to shop for furniture online. With the trackers, their activities and movements are tracked for further analysis.

























The Online Store Pages


The online store contains 5 subpages for shopping, a registration page, signup page and shopping cart. It uses React.JS with HTML, CSS and JavaScript, with a Node.js backend for collecting data.










`












The Eye Gaze Tracking and Instruction


For the tracking, users have to consent to the collecting of data, and be introduced to the description of the game, with a demo credit card, and open the camera permission. Then they can start acting like shopping for the study.










`








Security Awareness Study


The pixel points of where the users look at, and their mouse movements were tracked as x, y coordinates. It was then resembled again showing where they look or the mouse stopped on the screen. Twelve participants' data were collected. You can see how users interact differently with different habits. It is also for analyzing the security design of the form.
















For example, when registering for the webpage, do users look at the security hints, or see what they agree to by clicking the button carefully? It was measured by how long they gazed or the mouse cursor stopped on the elements.










Insight of the Human Computer Interaction


With the tracker and data, we can see if their mouse movements go along with their eye gaze, or if their mouse only stopped at one place.
